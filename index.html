What Is Digital Fabrication & Prototyping Fundamentals(EP1000) ?

EP1000 is a elective module offered at Singapore Polytechnic(SP). The elective is organised around a self-directed project where students can develop and showcase their design thinking and prototyping skills. The elective will teach students techniques to design and create their projects enabling them with skills such as Computer Aided Design, 3D Printing, Lazer Cutting and Arduino .
